## Saving Money: The 3 Pillars of ROI

### 1. Electricity Savings
A pump protected from dry-run conditions lasts significantly longer. Precision automation stops the pump the instant the tank is full, cutting down on the wasted electricity from every fill cycle.

### 2. Water Conservation
Every overflow event is water you paid to pump and watched run down the drain. Preventing overflow provides immediate savings on your water bill.

### 3. Maintenance Costs
Catching a problem before it becomes a failure is where the biggest savings show up. Emergency plumbing call-outs and motor repairs cost significantly more than scheduled maintenance.
